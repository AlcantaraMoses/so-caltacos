Asada (Steak)

An authentic steak taco is a classic Mexican dish that showcases tender, juicy slices of seasoned beef steak, nestled in a warm corn tortilla. The steak is seasoned with a blend of chili powder, paprika, cumin, garlic powder, and other spices, imparting rich and bold flavors to every bite.

Birria

features tender and flavorful slow-cooked meat, usually beef is shredded and nestled in a warm and soft corn tortilla. The meat is simmered in a rich and spicy broth made with chiles and a blend of spices, creating a juicy and flavorful filling with a hint of heat..

Al Pastor

features juicy and flavorful marinated pork, cooked on a spit and sliced thin to be nestled in a warm and soft corn tortilla. The pork is marinated with a blend of spices, including chili pepper, achiote, and pineapple, resulting in a savory and slightly sweet filling with a hint of heat. The tortilla is then topped with fresh toppings such as diced onions, cilantro, a squeeze of lime, and a slice of pineapple, balancing the flavors and textures in every bite.

What we do

Our taco catering service offers a variety of authentic and delicious tacos, made with the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes. Our menu includes a wide range of options, from classic beef and chicken tacos to spicy chorizo and al pastor tacos, to cater to all tastes and dietary requirements. We use only the best quality corn tortillas, which are grilled to perfection to create the perfect texture and flavor. In addition to our tacos, we also offer a selection of sides and appetizers to complement the main course, such as Mexican rice, refried beans, guacamole, and fresh salsa. Our presentation is visually appealing and professional, ensuring that your guests will be impressed not only with the taste but also with the appearance of our food. We understand the importance of delivering exceptional service and quality food, and our team of experienced chefs and servers will work closely with you to ensure that your event runs smoothly. Our goal is to make your event stress-free and enjoyable, so you can focus on enjoying the company of your guests. Our prices are competitive, and we can work within your budget to provide a customized menu that fits your needs. Please let us know if you have any special requests or dietary restrictions, and we will be happy to accommodate them.
