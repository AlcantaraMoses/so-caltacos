Asada (Steak)

An authentic steak taco is a classic Mexican dish that showcases tender, juicy slices of seasoned beef steak, nestled in a warm corn tortilla. The steak is seasoned with a blend of chili powder, paprika, cumin, garlic powder, and other spices, imparting rich and bold flavors to every bite.

Chorizo (Spicy Pork)

features spicy, flavorful chorizo sausage, wrapped in a warm and soft corn tortilla. The chorizo is made with a blend of ground pork and a special blend of spices, resulting in a robust and savory sausage with a kick of heat.

Birria

features tender and flavorful slow-cooked meat, usually beef is shredded and nestled in a warm and soft corn tortilla. The meat is simmered in a rich and spicy broth made with chiles and a blend of spices, creating a juicy and flavorful filling with a hint of heat..

Al Pastor

features juicy and flavorful marinated pork, cooked on a spit and sliced thin to be nestled in a warm and soft corn tortilla. The pork is marinated with a blend of spices, including chili pepper, achiote, and pineapple, resulting in a savory and slightly sweet filling with a hint of heat. The tortilla is then topped with fresh toppings such as diced onions, cilantro, a squeeze of lime, and a slice of pineapple, balancing the flavors and textures in every bite.

What we do

Our taco catering offers authentic and delicious tacos with fresh ingredients. Our menu includes a variety of options like classic beef, chicken, chorizo, and al pastor tacos. We use top-quality grilled corn tortillas. Sides include Mexican rice, refried beans, guacamole, and salsa. Our experienced team ensures exceptional service for a stress-free event. Competitive prices, customizable menus, and accommodation for special requests or dietary restrictions are available.
